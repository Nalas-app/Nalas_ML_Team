## 8. Recommendations

1. **Use 8-10 features** for the initial ML model (top 5 + business critical)
2. **Revisit demand features** once 100+ orders are collected (`historical_demand` likely becomes important)
3. **Add price history features** once 3+ months of price snapshots exist
4. **Monitor feature drift** — if top feature rankings change significantly, retrain model
5. **The rule-based MVP is well-calibrated** — ingredient cost + quantity already explain 88% of variance
