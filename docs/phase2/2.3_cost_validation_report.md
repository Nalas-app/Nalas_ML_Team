## 1. Cost Calculation Methodology

### Formula Applied:
```
ingredient_cost    = SUM(ingredient[i].quantity × ingredient[i].price × wastage_factor)
labor_cost         = ingredient_cost × 0.15
overhead_cost      = ingredient_cost × 0.10
wastage_cost       = ingredient_cost × 0.05
subtotal           = ingredient_cost + labor_cost + overhead_cost + wastage_cost
profit_margin      = subtotal × 0.10
final_cost         = subtotal + profit_margin
```

### Multipliers Applied:
| Component | Rate | Basis |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Labor | 15% | of ingredient cost |
| Overhead | 10% | of ingredient cost |
| Wastage | 5% | of ingredient cost |
| Profit | 10% | of subtotal |
| Wastage Factor | 1.05x | per ingredient quantity |
